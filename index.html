How to create music

Creating music can be difficult if you don't know music theory, all you need is perfect pitch and an understanding on where to put notes. My number one rule is to occasionally bend the "rules" of music theory.

    The basics

  1. The basic C major scale is C-D-E-F-G-A-B, while not not the best at piano, I know those will always be on the white keys.
  2. C minor is C-D-(D#/Eb)-F-G-(G#/Ab)-(A#/Bb). It uses 3 black keys and # is a sharp note and b is a flat note.
